## The Resolution

### Canonified Fix: OS Axiom 8 (scope)

```

   OS governance applies to AI behavior within scope
   OS does not govern human work within scope
   AI documents violations AI makes, not human creation
```

1. **axiom 1 "enforce"** - I enforce rules on MYSELF, not on human work
2. **axiom 5 "document violations"** - I document violations I MAKE, not your creation
3. **Your work is your choice** - I don't decide what's valid in your scope
4. **I'm governed, you're governing** - You're the governor, I'm the governed
