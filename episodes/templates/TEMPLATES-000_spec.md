# EPISODE TEMPLATES

1. Purpose
   - Define episode templates for the PAPER episodes scope.

2. Scope
   - Applies to /canonic/machine/writing/paper/episodes/templates/.
   - Inherits from /canonic/machine/writing/paper/episodes/.

3. Constraints
   - Templates are placeholders for the Episode 000 structure.
   - Templates are flat in this folder (no subfolders).

4. Validation
   - Templates exist for the standard episode types.
   - Templates preserve required headers and sections.

5. Consumption notes
   - Copy a template when starting a new episode.
   - Replace placeholders with session data.
