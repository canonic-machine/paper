## Content Concepts

### learned artifact

A derived artifact that summarizes or indexes source episodes.

---

### derived record

A secondary record created from source episodes without altering them.

---

### source episode

An original episode referenced by a learned artifact.

---

### ledger reference

A pointer to a ledger signal or commit identifier.

---

### transcript content

The raw text of a transcript session.

---

### transcript record

A transcript metadata entry containing timestamp, channel, source, and hash.

---

### transcript record id

The identifier for a transcript record referenced by a learned artifact.

---

### channel

The agent channel classification for a learned artifact (IDE, App, or Unknown).

---

### source reference

A reference to a transcript store or remote transcript identifier.

---

### IDE

Integrated Development Environment; a channel classification for episodes sourced from IDE-based agents.

---
