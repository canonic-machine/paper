## Content Concepts

### content

The text of an episode artifact.

---

### index

A list of episode references.

---

### interpretation

The assignment of meaning to artifacts.

---

### reference

A pointer to an episode identifier or file path.

---

### transcript store

The local log directory used to capture session transcripts for a tool.

---

### stream

A directory that indexes episodes by type or channel.

---

### channel

The agent channel classification for an episode index entry (IDE, App, or Unknown).

---

### IDE

Channel for episodes sourced from IDE-based agents and transcript stores.

---

### App

Channel for episodes sourced from App-based transcripts or API sessions.

---

### source

A source reference for an episode entry (store name, path, or remote transcript ID).

---

### unknown

Channel for episodes without enough evidence to classify as IDE or App.

---

### type

A label in episode metadata used for classification.

---

### session

A bounded period of agent activity within a channel. Sessions are the atomic unit of transcript capture.

---
